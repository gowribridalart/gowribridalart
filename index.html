About

Gowri Bridal Mehndi is a premium bridal henna service based in Chennai, offering exquisite and customized mehndi designs for weddings, engagements, baby showers, and festive occasions. With years of experience and a passion for traditional artistry, Gowri specializes in intricate bridal mehndi, Arabic, Rajasthani, Indo-western, and contemporary henna patterns.

We use skin-safe, chemical-free henna to ensure rich, long-lasting color and beautiful results. Whether it’s full-hand bridal mehndi or elegant designs for friends and family, Gowri creates timeless art tailored to your style.
